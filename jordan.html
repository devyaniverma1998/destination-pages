Jordan

Jordan is a land of breathtaking contrasts—ancient cities carved in stone, dramatic desert valleys, serene seas, and welcoming hospitality. Imagine wandering through the rose-red city of Petra, floating effortlessly in the healing waters of the Dead Sea, or gazing at endless stars in Wadi Rum’s desert skies. From the vibrant streets of Amman to the timeless ruins of Jerash, every moment here is a blend of history, culture, and wonder.

Jordan Travel Guide

Welcome to Jordan

Jordan is a land of ancient wonders and striking desert landscapes—from the rose-red city of Petra to the star-filled skies of Wadi Rum. Float in the mineral-rich waters of the Dead Sea, dive or snorkel among coral reefs in Aqaba on the Red Sea, and wander through Roman streets in Jerash. Here, timeless heritage, warm Bedouin hospitality, and modern comfort come together to create a journey you’ll never forget.

Dead Sea & Red Sea

Experience two legendary waters in one trip. The Dead Sea offers soothing mineral muds and the surreal sensation of floating, while Aqaba on the Red Sea invites you to relax on the beach or explore vibrant coral reefs by snorkeling, diving, or glass-bottom boat. Whether wellness or water sports, Jordan’s shores deliver unforgettable moments.

Cities & Heritage

Begin in Amman with its bustling cafés, citadel views, and Roman Theater. Trace mosaic masterpieces in Madaba, stand on Mount Nebo’s historic viewpoint, and step into living history among the colonnades of Jerash. Each stop blends tradition and contemporary life—revealing Jordan’s welcoming character.

Desert & Adventure

Journey to Wadi Rum’s cinematic dunes and sandstone cliffs for 4×4 safaris, camel rides, hiking, or sandboarding—then stargaze at night from a Bedouin camp. Trek the Dana Biosphere Reserve or try canyoning in Wadi Mujib when conditions allow. Adventure in Jordan is as epic as its landscapes.

Resorts & Stays

From luxury spa resorts along the Dead Sea to chic hotels in Amman, beachfront escapes in Aqaba, and atmospheric Bedouin camps in Wadi Rum, Jordan offers stays for every style. Expect comfort, character, and genuine Jordanian hospitality—because where you stay is part of the story.
