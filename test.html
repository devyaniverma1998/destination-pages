Vietnam

Vietnam isn’t about polished perfection—it’s the steaming bowls of pho at a sidewalk stall, the motorbike chaos in Hanoi, the quiet mornings in Hoi An’s lantern-lit streets. Ever sipped coffee so strong it wakes your soul? That’s Vietnam—unfiltered, intense, and deeply human. The beaches? Sure, they’re stunning, but the magic’s in the mess: the laughter of market vendors, the scent of lemongrass, the way time slows in the highlands.
